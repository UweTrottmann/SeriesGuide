SeriesGuide: show manager

Keep track of your favorite TV shows and movies.

SeriesGuide is a free app to discover TV shows and movies, keep track of your watched episodes and movies, and get notified about upcoming episodes.

<b>Discover TV shows and movies</b> that are available on your streaming services, new, popular or similar to those you are watching.

<b>Check off and keep track</b> of watched episodes and movies. <b>Find where to watch</b> a TV show or movie. Stay aware of new episodes.

<b>Back up and sync</b> your TV shows, lists and movies across devices using SeriesGuide Cloud.

<b>Connect to Trakt</b> to sync your watched and collected episodes and movies, access your history and see what others are watching. Post ratings and comments.

<b>No account required</b> to use the app. If you want, you can back up and restore your library using JSON files only.

<b>Open source and independent</b>: SeriesGuide is supported by its subscribers, not by advertisers or investors.

For support, questions or more information visit https://www.seriesgui.de/help

To check out the source code, visit https://github.com/UweTrottmann/SeriesGuide
