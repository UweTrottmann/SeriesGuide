--- layout: help title: Manually update shows ---

ℹ️ Shows are updated automatically by default. If not, check your settings.

To update a single show:

  1. From the show list, tap the three dots next to a show to display more options.
  2. Select Update.

To update all shows:

  1. From the show list, tap the three dots in the top app bar to display more options.
  2. Select Sync manually and then Sync & Update.

ℹ️ You can force a re-download of all shows (regardless of if they were just updated, which may take a long time) using Sync & Download all.
