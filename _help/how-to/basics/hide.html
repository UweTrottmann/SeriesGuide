--- layout: help title: "Make a hidden show visible again" ---
  1. From the Added list, tap the filter symbol in the top app bar.
  2. Change the Hidden filter to Included and all other filters to Disabled to only display hidden shows.
  3. Find the show to make visible again, tap the three dots to display more options.
  4. Tap Do not hide.
  5. Restore your previous filter settings or use Reset all filters to restore the default list view.
