--- layout: help title: "Change or reset the password used for SeriesGuide Cloud" ---

If you have signed up for SeriesGuide Cloud using email and password, to change or reset your password:

  1. In the bottom bar, select More and then SeriesGuide Cloud.
  2. Then sign out of your account.
  3. Then start to sign in again using email. But when asked to enter a password, select Trouble signing in? and send yourself instructions to reset your password.
  4. Once completed, you can sign in with the new password.
