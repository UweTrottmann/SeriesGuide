--- layout: help title: Cancel, change or refund a subscription ---

If you have subscribed through Google Play, you can cancel, change or in some regions for a brief time ask for a refund yourself.

You can also contact me to issue a refund. To do so, first find out the email address of the account used to complete the purchase, or the order number. Then send them to me and ask for a refund.

ℹ️ For purchases on Google Play, you can find the order number and invoice on the Google Payments website. Make sure to sign in with the account that was used to complete the purchase.
